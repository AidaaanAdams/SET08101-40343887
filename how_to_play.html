Survivor

How to play!

On each page you will be given a scenario and it is upto you to decide the future of your character.
You will be given different options on each page so you can shape your story entirely, choose carefully, or you might end up dead!
If you made the wrong choice you can always use the go back button to change your story and stop your brains from being eaten!
